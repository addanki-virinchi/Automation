Career Guidance

19 Jan, 8:30 PM

Generative AI is no longer a niche skill — it is rapidly becoming a core hiring requirement for high-paying tech roles. However, most learners feel overwhelmed: What to learn? In what order? Which skills actually matter for 30 LPA+ roles? This session is designed to answer those questions with absolute clarity.

 

In “From Zero to 30 LPA+ GenAI Role: Complete Roadmap for 2026”, Sumit Shukla — Director & AI Head at Scalertix.ai — shares a practical, industry-tested roadmap to break into top GenAI roles, even if you’re starting from scratch. Having worked closely with enterprise AI systems, hiring teams, and real-world GenAI deployments, Sumit brings a no-fluff, reality-driven perspective.

 

The session starts with a reality check: learning random tools or just prompt engineering is not enough. Recruiters evaluate candidates on fundamentals, applied GenAI systems, project depth, and problem-solving ability. This roadmap cuts through the noise and shows exactly what separates average candidates from those landing 30 LPA+ offers.

 

What You’ll Learn
